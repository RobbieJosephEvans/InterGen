InterGen - What We Do

Through the use of a dynamic website that can create accounts, usernames, and posts, InterGen connects senior citizens in hospitals and senior homes with high school students. Human interaction is essential for the good health of people but sometimes difficult to find as senior citizens. Whether families and friends are busy or far away, there are a variety of reasons why some seniors are alone and lacking interaction. This can be detrimental to one's emotional and even physical health, and symptoms are amplified in hospitals and senior homes. For this reason, InterGen provides a platform for students to visit seniors in retirement homes.
